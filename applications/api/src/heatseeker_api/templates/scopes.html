Research scope

Tunable industry, geography, and research-purpose targeting. The active scope filters sources now, and will drive discovery, leads, and market outputs in later milestones. The scopes below are just saved presets — a scope can combine anything: named regions, countries, states, and cities together (e.g. ANZ, NORTH_AMERICA, AU-VIC-MELBOURNE), plus exclusions to carve areas out (e.g. all of APAC except CN). Codes are hierarchical: a national source (AU) still covers a state scope (AU-QLD), and state sources count toward a national scope.
